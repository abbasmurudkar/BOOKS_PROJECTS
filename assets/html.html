TILAK EDUCATION SOCIETY

Tilak College of Science and Commerce was started in the year 2002 - 2003 with B.Sc. (C.S) and B.Com. In the year 2003-2004 the college added B.M.S., B.M.M., B.Sc. (I.T) and B.Sc. (Bio-Tech). BSc (Microbiology) was started in 2006-2007.The college has started post graduation in science this year in MSc(Computer Science) and MSc(Information Technology).The college also plans to start MSc(Biotechnology) this academic year.
